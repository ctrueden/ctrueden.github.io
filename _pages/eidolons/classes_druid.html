Druid

Druids revere the land itself. Though they respect the ley lines and nexuses as a portion of that power, they look to the more fundamental elements for their inspiration, rather than specializing in spiritual nexus energy or flow manipulation like geomancers do. It is believed in druidic lore than the land itself is the most ancient and primal of powers.
