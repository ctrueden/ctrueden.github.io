Private Details - Iraja Neviah Beag

[Character sheet]

 

Spellthief

  • You name a spell, of a level within your abilities to steal
  • If the target knows that spell, they lose access to it for one minute, they lose an amount of vitality equal to the spell's vitality cost, and you add that amount of vitality to your spellpool. (Normally, a spellthief's spellpool is empty and does not automatically refill, but stealing a spell fills it up temporarily.) You also gain the ability to cast the stolen spell once.
  • When you steal a spell, you target the enemy's spellpool first.
  • If they do not know the spell you named, you get a random spell of the same level instead.
