Iraja Neviah Beag (ee ruh juh - neh VYE ah - bek)

Mystic Nomad

600 yrs old

Extremely androgynous, fit Halfling; perhaps exuding more female characteristics.  Dressed in traveling clothes and leather armor.  Black hair cut in a very short and interesting style, that only allows the hair to slightly reveal its waviness.  Very large, deep dark grey-green eyes.

Nomadic Prophet Gone Astray

My name is Iraja Neviah Beag and I was born to Nomads Iliriana Aleta Neviah of the Baenallia Tribe and Rajasi Kritnu Beag of the Gearnon Tribe.  They are both strong, curious, and small people.  They travel less distance exploring than they used to but they continue to build strong bonds with their kin and kith.  They taught me to follow the path to knowledge, stand by what I believe, as well as how to adjust embrace and enhance the flow around us.

I myself am a nomadic Geomancer Spellthief.  My appearance suggests full maturity of about 30 years.  I can tell you that I have been here many more years than that but not near as many as the ancient ones.  I grew as a seer and prophet with the Baenallian people.  I always had a knack for gaining strength and insight from the ley lines and nexus points.  Chazona Aindri Clem is a very ancient seer who was of my people but also travels alone much of the time and has deep connections with many tribes.  I was fortunate enough to gain her respect and be able to learn from her how to embrace the prophecies and retell them without bias or incorrect interpretation.  I studied with her for nearly fifty years.  She was am amazing teacher, mentor and will always be a great friend, I hope.  She was a great relief and support for me when our brethren broke from us about two hundred years past.  I especially did not take my old friend and cousin Sevran Kritnu Baenanís departure very well at first.  Chazona helped me to see that the difference in interpretation was not necessarily wrong and that this was truly an example of the prophecies showing themselves through odd paths.

Eventually I learned that the earth could not only give me power, but so could other earth dwellers.  Some people do not like this flow of power nearly so much.  As a prophet I merely told them what the Earth was whispering and they knew I was just a messenger.  With the ability to literally take from others one becomes a threat in the wrong circumstances.  I have therefore always used my power with respect and good intent, especially with my kin and kith.

Not long ago things turned very much for me.  I began to experience a most deep feeling of dread and the prophecies started coming to me even more often, in my waking and sleeping hours.  There were different though, broken and allusive in meaning.  I feel that they are all linked together as some huge story.  All that I have gotten from it so far is that the nexuses are at risk, and should not be guarded and controlled as they are. For the first time I am ridden with so much fear that even after great thought I feel that we can no longer stand by, action is finally needed.

I was respected by my people and one day it was thought that I would even be a great elder.  Since I have started to see these dreadful things no one trusts me.  Iliriana and Rajasi seem to have lost respect and pride for me.   And my closest friend, Glwys Aiken Jager, has actually turned his back on me.  It is almost as if he forgot all the rest of his heritage and the strength of bonds of kith for the belief in inaction.  I am now traveling more alone and with more of a purpose than I have ever before in my life. I feel that I need to seek out more answers and that a conference with Chazona once again would be greatly warranted.
