And in other news...

It's a sad day for the Imperial war effort against the barbaric and honorless Hordes, as the City of Manavendra has been sacked, razed to the ground after a many-month long siege. No news has been heard from the ruling Narhari family since the Horde's final push - not from canny Deva Prime Minister Shardul, his arcanist wife "Queen" Archita, their daughter, Lady Sachi, or even spoiled-but-skilled princling Lord Vashir; all attempts at scrying them have failed. In the face of the lack of evidence, and the century of prosperity and victory they brought Manavendra, as well as their efforts to rid us of the scourge of the Nexii, they will be presumed dead with honor until further notice.

Not all news is glum, however - the disproportionate effort the Hordes spent on Manavendra allowed General Nephron to launch a devastating foray into Horde lands...
