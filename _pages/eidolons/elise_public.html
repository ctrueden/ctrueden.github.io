Public Profile - Gwenvyth Trieleffyn Tal'Arabep

Player: 
Elise
Name: 
Gwenvyth Trieleffyn Tal'Arabep
Group: 
Red
Gender: 
Female
Alignment: 
Lawful evil
Race: 
Elf/Drow
Class: 
Bard & Cleric/Dirgesinger

"Gwen" Tal'Arabep has no qualms about flaunting her nobility.  A well-fed elf, she stands tall in lush robes, her nose in the air; but, careful, lilac eyes don't miss much of the world around her.  She is well aware that her gray skin and oh, so carefully plaited white hair sets her apart from other elves.  So envied by the gods were her kin that they were cursed to suffer in beholding the majesty and beauty of the sky-kissed world--the privilege of refreshing their immortality is agonizing, though, a small price to pay for perfection.  Blessed in the arcane, necromantic, divine, and material arts, it is the duty of the Tal'Arabep clan to chronicle for the unfortunate masses their keen understanding of true beauty.

She wears a heavy, black cloak and carries a slender, cherrywood lute banded with silver.  With her hair piled loosely on her head in a feathered clip, one can see three runes branded at the nape of her neck.  Her deep red robes are not exactly practical for travel but are nonetheless immaculate.  All her jewlery is wrought in bone, gems, and silver and she wears an intricate necklace strung from the engraved and laquered skeletons of some tiny animal.
