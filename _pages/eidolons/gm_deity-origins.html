Deity origins

Posted in
  • Needs work

Pre-war only:

  • Melora - had Ehlonna's position
  • Grazzt (DR -) - technically not a god—part of the Abyss, which is subject to the flow
  • Tharizdun (DR 20) - god of humans, now enslaved

 

Deities (all DR 10 unless otherwise noted):

  • Avandra - Good - freedom, trade, adventure, travel, frontier
  • Bahamut - Lawful Good - justice, protection, nobility, honor
  • Corellon - Unaligned - spring, beauty, the arts, arcane magic, fey
  • Erathis - Unaligned - great invention, founder of cities, author of laws
  • Io - Unaligned - true dragon god, composed of Bahamut and Tiamat
  • Ioun - Unaligned - knowledge, skill and prophecy
  • Kord - Unaligned - storm god, lord of battle
  • Moradin - Lawful Good - artisans, miners, smiths, dwarves
  • Pelor - Good - sun, summer, time
  • Sehanine - Unaligned - moon, autumn, trickery, illusions, Lolth's sister
    • Corellon and Lolth were lovers -- Corellon cast Lolth aside when she got uppity -- then he fell in love with Lolth's sister Sehanine and eventually elevated her to godhood.
  • Bane - war and conquest, god of goblins
  • Gruumsh - chaotic evil - destruction, barbarian hordes, orcs
  • Lolth - chaotic evil - shadow, lies, spiders, scheming, treachery
    • See Sehanine above
  • Tiamat - evil - wealth, greed, envy
    • Possibly ate Bahamut
  • Boccob - neutral - magic, knowledge
    • Double of Vecna
  • Ehlonna - neutral good - goddess of woodlands
  • Erythnul - chaotic evil - slaughter, panic
  • Fharlanghn - neutral - god of roads, travel, distance, horizons
  • Garl Glittergold - neutral good - gnomes, joker, watchful protector, priceless gem, sparkling wit
  • Heironeous - lawful good - valor
    • Hextor's double
  • Obad-Hai - neutral - nature, wilderness and storms
  • Wee Jas - lawful neutral - death and magic
    • Original goddess of death, enemy of Nerull and the Raven Queen, who she feels have usurped portions of her authority/portfolio
    • Judged those who die to determine their fate, but the Raven Queen has usurped that duty from her
  • Yondalla - lawful good - halflings


Post-war:

  • Asmodeus - tyranny and domination (DR 15)
    • A version of Lucifer who attained divinity from the Temple of Sigil
    • By creating dead worlds, she forged the Hells (of which there are currently Nine)
    • The Hells exist outside the flow (those worlds have been ripped from it), and hence there is only one Asmodeus
    • She may be capable of using avatars but has never done so; it may not be possible unless she traveled to Prime, since the Hells are so isolated
    • However, there are portals from the Hells back to the mortal realm
  • Demogorgon - lord of the Abyss (DR -)
    • Two versions of Lucifer who combined and entered Tharizdun's mind to attempt to free him
    • They failed, becoming twisted by Tharizdun's hatred (negative energy), and thus the Abyss was created
    • The Abyss is subject to the flow, so Demogorgon lives within it rather than using avatars
  • Vecna - evil - necromancy, secrets, forbidden knowledge (DR 5 currently)
    • Double of Boccob
  • Hextor - lawful evil - tyranny
    • Heironeous's double and rival
  • Kiaransalee, the Lady of Pain (DR 20) - lawful neutral
    • A version of Lucifer who, having illicitly attained godhood from Tharizdun as his final conscious act, was forcibly bound to serve the other gods as the Lady of Pain
    • She is largely insane, but has cordoned off portions of her mind to act semi-sanely
    • One of "Tharizdun's Trinity"
    • She has DR 20 because she was created by the others each sacrificing a piece of themselves; so other gods are all half normal DR, maximum 10
  • Ghaunadaur, the One Who Waits (DR 20) - neutral evil
    • A version of Lucifer who retained divine stature by hiding in Kiaransalee's necklace
    • That which lurks, the elder eye
    • Biding its time until it can strike against the gods themselves
    • Portfolio allows it to hide from the eyes of the other deities
    • One of "Tharizdun's Trinity"
  • St. Cuthbert - lawful neutral - retribution (DR 20)
    • A gestalt entity, composed of Kiaransalee and Ghaunadaur
    • Her avatar has been known as Kas
      • attacks strategically to maximize destined forces of the gods' destruction
      • freed Haroun [kelsey]
      • freed Kithlandil [paul]
    • One of "Tharizdun's Trinity"
  • The Raven Queen (DR 20)
    • One of two semi-sane aspects of Kiaransalee
    • Neutral – god of death, spinner of fate, patron of winter
    • Responsible for the plane of shadow
    • Champion of the shadar kai (shadow humans)
    • Judges those who die to determine their fate
    • One of her avatars in the shadow realm was known as Jennifer
  • Nerull - evil god of death (DR 20) - neutral evil
    • One of two semi-sane aspects of Kiaransalee
    • Responsible for the undead rising from the withering
    • Seeks to destroy all mortal life, but not without purpose
  • Olidammara - chaotic neutral trickster (DR 10+10+10+...=?)
    • Garl's twins
    • Garl was copied in the initial copying—that copy was the original Olidammara
    • That copy was then copied again in the second copying (unlike Garl), making two Olidammaras
    • Those two copies were then copied yet again in the final copying as the Temple was invoked, making four
    • So there were four Olidammaras for quite some time
    • Olidammara figured out how to hide his divine essence from those who could normally see it, such as other gods and the Guild of the Invisible Hand
    • As a joke, one version of him eventually joined the Guild, but the ritual to make him a Brethren altered his nature unexpectedly—losing his memory and becoming totally formless, his drive to proliferate himself took a front seat and he became the kitsune race
    • Two awakened kitsune, Shiki and Shinkumo, work together, one wearing the laughing rogue's smiling mask, the other wearing the crying one


Domains:

  • Darkness domain - Raven Queen, Lolth, Ghaunadaur
  • Luck domain - Asmodeus, Garl Glittergold, Olidammara

Lady of Pain is Lucifer

  • The One Who Waits is hiding in the Lady of Pain's necklace
  • She pulled him in as she was being transformed
  • She knows he is there, and he voluntarily stays there to remain in hiding
  • The "trail went cold" shortly after the gods made her
  • They questioned her, but she told them nothing -- she can't speak
  • She is so screwy that she doesn't respond directly to questions -- too scary
  • She can resist more orders than they believe

Boccob has knowledge and secrets in his portfolio

  • Countered by the One Who Waits, so he doesn't know who the Raven Queen or St. Cuthbert are (and doesn't know if the One Who Waits is real either)
