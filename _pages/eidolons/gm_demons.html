Celestial and Abyssal realms

The Celestial and Abyssal realms are defined within Tharizdun, in a sense. The nexuses are portals there. Demogorgon created the first inverted nexus at the Temple he used to become a deity, and entered it, hoping to free Tharizdun, but instead reached the Elemental Chaos, where his presence inadvertently created the Abyss.

 

"Angels"

On the other hand, angels could appear through the positive nexuses, but have not yet done so. Mortals "diseased" with too much positive nexus energy become pseudonatural, and begin to mutate toward a tainted celestial nature.

These two extremes represent the two aspects of Tharizdun's insanity: Heaven & Hell. Only by finding a balance between them can true sanity be achieved.

Deva & rakshasa

Devas and rakshasa are the two sides of Tharizdun's personality

  • both sides represent madness, but of opposing sorts
  • if you fall too far into hell fist, you become a rakshasa
  • if you master both heaven & hell fist, you cease being either, and return to being a human affected by the flow
  • gods recognize that those humans are avatar fragments of Tharizdun
  • (gods can recognize each others' avatars, unless portfolio allows concealment)

 

The heaven & hell fist fighting styles were invented by humans during the war of the gods, by drawing on the sealed power of Tharizdun. Their use created rakshasa and deva—people who overused one or the other began to transform. Kiaransalee was the first one to fall to the Hell Fist and become a demon: Demogorgon. Vhaeraun (Za'har), in an attempt to counterbalance Kiaransalee, pursued the Heaven Fist and became the first deva.
