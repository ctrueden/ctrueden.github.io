F-03: Blue

Note: Tribe of orcs with the flaming hammer sigil (with teeth!) are known as the Razortooth clan.

 

Imminent events

  • Party is deciding where to go next. They could go into the Wilds directly, or they could keep backtracking the orc army.
    • If they go to Intempestivus, they could learn about the teleportation events a while ago.
      • Strange night a few weeks back when the high vizier of Arx registered a concern about the teleporter pads.
      • Use of the teleporter pads by non-authorized personnel can result in "teleportatoin sickness"...
    • If they go into the Wilds, they are attacked (initially) by a platypus warrior, but can make friends.
  • Either way, if they are looking for someone who knows about the Wilds, they learn of an oracle living in the swamp north of the lake.
  • Secretly, this is an ancient female black dragon (Lady Mordollwen).
  • At Mordollwen, they face enemies (combats—swamp hags, chuuls, others?).
  • Somewhere in here, while information gathering, or while trekking through Mordollwen, the Red quake hits and players switch:
    • Lose Sahrit and Biger, gain Arwel (with Sword) and Haroun.
    • Switch GMs!?

 

After the quake

  • They eventually find the oracle's lair in the swamp. It is a beautiful palace filled with treasure... and the oracle appears as a beautiful, ageless woman—some kind of genasi, maybe? But her skin, hair and eyes are all raven black.
  • She is flirtatious, especially with Haroun and Arwel (because she has the Sword).
  • Anyway, they need to find a way to appease her without her eating them:
    • Give me that weapon (the Sword)!
    • Or the heart of a nexus... (if they mention extraction techniques: "better, teach me how to extract them myself.")
    • Bring me the heart of the creature who dwells in the depths of the Frozen Wood.
    • Bring me Mjollnir, the hammer of the Storm Giant King.
    • Lastly, if they can form an initial bond with her, using Diplomacy or other empathic attitude: find me a mate, who will live here with me henceforth.
      • That one (Haroun) is intriguing—he stays with me.
      • Or, if they call her on being a dragon, she asks for a draconic mate.
  • Once the dragon is appeased, it may reveal:
    • Dragon is wary (i.e., afraid) to go deep into the Wilds, even though that is her homeland.
    • She cannot leave the swamp for too long, because most nexuses are not powerful enough to sustain her.
    • She comes from the northern reach, far upstream along the Yára river.
    • She knows that even deeper along the river lies something of immense magnitude—a place of death. She laughs as she reveals this, knowing the party faces death if they pursue their goal.
