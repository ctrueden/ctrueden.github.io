F-04: Blue

During their journey to Mordollwen, they are ambushed by Haft ("the black private dick who's a sex machine to all the chicks"), Talibah (a genasi geomancer prophetess) and Index (a warforged who is good at finding things):

  • on the road as they skirt around the lake
  • or on a ship if they try to cross the lake that way
  • they got there first using phantom steeds
  • knew where to go because Talibah divined the party's destination using prophecy
  • Talibah and Index fight to incapacitate, but will kill if necessary, except for Haroun
  • probably brought a handful of other mercenaries with them to help fight

Once they defeat this enemy and reach the swamp, they will run into the red group having just finished the swamp encounter. See Joint 4 for details beyond this point.
