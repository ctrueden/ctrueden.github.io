While Isaline is on the shadow path, she receives a raven messenger from Corvidae! And the message cannot be intercepted by Vecna, because this plane is the domain of the Raven Queen. The message reads:

Dear Isaline,

Apologies for my silence, but I have been dead for the past five years. At that time, Soren van Wagram assassinated me when I uncovered proof, in the form of a letter, that he was responsible for the murder of Marquis Frederick. I also learned that he is a member of a necromantic cult, and apparently possesses significant power in that regard. Van Wagram is extremely dangerous—do not confront him until you can strike from a position of strength.

Since my death I have discovered that Van Wagram's plots extend significantly farther than either of us had thought. In my prior communication I mentioned a place of great power in the Wilds. I have received confirmation that Van Wagram and his ilk now seek this place as well. Thus, I urge you to find it before he and his allies do.

Your path will lead you to a person knowledgeable about the Wilds, and thus capable of assisting in this regard. But beware: she is a deadly enemy if crossed, and will require much from you and your allies in exchange for her help. Best of luck in your endeavours—I do not exaggerate when I say that the fate of the world may hang in the balance of your actions. I sincerely hope we will meet again one day.

With warmest regards,
Corvidae

P.S. I've taken the liberty of reviving your slain ally. Give my regards to Barnabas.

After they reach the end of the path, they find themselves in a swamp, where they meet a strange woman. The green hag pretends to be the oracle (disguise self) surrounded by two glowing balls of light (actually will-o'-wisps), but when the party drops their guard, she and her allies attack them:

  • Green Hag—strength damage with a touch: 2d4 (DC 16 Fort negates)
  • Will-O'-Wisp x2—shock 2d8 electricity
  • Chuul x2—hit with claw, get free grapple
  • Eight-Headed Hydra x1—attack with all heads! and AoO!

After defeating these guardians, they see the magnificant palace visible through the murk in the distance. This is Mordollwen's palace.

See Session 4: Joint for details after this point.
