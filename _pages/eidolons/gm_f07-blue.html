F-07: Blue

Talk to Grazzt -- charming and seductive, but slimy

Grazzt's next move: work his wiles on the leaders of Delphia.

"I am one of your people"

Terminus is now a portal to the Abyss

Silius Vartarra wants to know whether it can be used as a portal

He wants to use Grazzt, but mistrusts him of course

Grazzt wants to insinuate himself into the Delphian trust enough that they believe him to be an ally, for whatever reason.

"I come to serve."

Why? "I am answering the call."

Who called you? "The rulers of this place."

How did we call you? "You opened the portal; therefore I am bound to your will. Do you not understand the nature of this arrangement?"

We decline. "There is no going back. We are here to serve."

Grazzt: "By coming forth in servitude, I bring with me all of the legions of the Abyss."

If Kith does not immediately flee, and Grazzt notices him (he always sees invisible and has +42 to Perception), he will immediately kill or capture Kith.

A discussion ensues among the Delphian leaders (which, if the party is clever/interested enough, they can be present at), about what to do. Some argue that they could finally beat back the Empire with this army, preserving their way of life. Others argue that they cannot trust the demons not to turn on them at the opportune moment.

When Sahrit tries to switch: it doesn't work, and he feels overwhelmed by some force of purity on the other side -- he feels the hand retreat ever so little from its journey towards his heart (regains one corruption point), and switches to Good.

Once they take the flying machine: 3 young adult green dragons hassle them on their way.

Eventually it stops working because it cannot withstand the wild magic.
