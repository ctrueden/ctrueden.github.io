Wild magic

Magic items crafted using nexuses are fine. But when casting spells, you must make a concentration roll (DC 10 + spell level + wild magic thickness), or the spell goes awry as determined by the GM.

Wild magic thickness:

  • 5 – Area where green dragons attack
  • 10 – Deeper than that
  • 15 – Echidnox villages (five, evenly spaced around the temple)
  • 20 – Arcadia (the Temple of Sigil)

The villages are not visible from over the canopy, but when they land the ship, they are attacked by two Echidnox scouts, defending their territory.

 

Echidnox and Platypods

Nomads originally seeded from a group assigned to protect the temple, and getting driven away when it became too dangerous to stay (Tarrasques showed up). Since then, many more Fey have joined. The Echidnox remember and consider themselves the temple's guardians, and have settled a few hours' distance on foot away. The Platypods also remember, but hate the Echidnox for abandoning their shared nomadic culture in favor of returning to Arcadia, the "place of death." That is, the Platypods no longer believe in protecting Arcadia from anything.

The Echidnox have learned to irrigate ley lines, creating a lattice upon which their ironwood village is grown. The trees are shaped into deadly shapes, turning the entire village into a living weapon. Echidnox warriors do leave the village to hunt, but only when necessary—often, monsters attack the village and the Echidnox use the defenses to defeat them, which combined with their local farming efforts yields plenty of food.

Also among their tribe are the Oni, mostly ogre magi. Some live among the Echidnox, some travel with the Platypods. They look red-skinned and mean usually, but when celebrating they wear ceremonial skull masks with colorful tassels.

The Echidnox respect endurance and general hardiness—anyone can kill, but to be strong enough not to be killed is something to strive for. The Platypods, in contrast, have cultivated their ability to kill using their deadly poisons.

All of the Echidnox have animal companions. All of the Oni have fiendish familiars.
