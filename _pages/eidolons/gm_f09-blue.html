BLUE GROUP - Nov 11

Who wrote session notes for Blue #6 and #7?

Village, chieftain, celebration

QUAKE:
-LOSE: Isaline, Gwenvyth
-GAIN: Rebin, Sahrit

PLAYERS: Andy, Jason, Jeff, John, Nate, Ryan

Elder Tarrasque must be avoided to get into the Temple itself
-Guards the temple's entrance
-Is Colossal+ size -- too big to go inside :-)
-Has epic pseudonatural template
-Can shoot 12 spines per round and use its breath weapon, which is a cone of overcharged positive nexus energy (Fort save DC 30 partial -- half damage and not taking on the pseudonatural properties)
-It is chameleonic -- after sniffing for them, it disappears as it readies itself

Tower is the one cursed by Asmodeus

Simone sees:
-Lucifer (Asmodeus) gaining power from the Temple by sitting within the ring at the top of the Spire
--describe her as an erinyes without wings; when she gets the powers, wings sprout forth from her back
--cursing the temple not part of the vision
-Two Lucifers (Demogorgon) merging into one entity, then leveling the temple
--the combined being opens some kind of portal within the ring
--the ley lines climbing the Spire twist and constrict, and appear as chains of energy -- they squeeze and destroy the spire as she disappears into the portal
-"something blocked" around "that woman"
-The Temple's construction
--Many people in a circle, ley lines weaving together, Spire grows from the ground, vision ends, Simone is blind

Writing at the temple is in Primal: a strange mix of Celestial, Abyssal and Infernal. Or rather, perhaps a common ancestor to those... It bears the look of runes of power strung together to form sentences.

Wrin:
-Looking at Ed, says "inferior model"
-Looking at Rebin, says "hate"
-Looking at the doors, says "authorized personnel only"

Set into the base of the Spire is a massive set of double doors.
-Gotta distract the Elder Tarrasque long enough to figure out how to open them
-Only gods and Lady of Pain can open the doors
-Written above the doors: "Within this place lies the tomb of the Betrayer"
-Doors say in Primal: "Only the Gods may enter here"
-Three PCs have a slice of divine essence -- if all three touch the doors together, they will react and open
-Some PCs are too related to Tharizdun, and will be repelled hard by the doors: Simone, Joe, Za'har, Isaline, Sahrit-without-the-Hand

Temple is inhabited by devils, which Asmodeus sent to protect it
-Their goal is to kill anyone who enters the temple
-They are ready for the party
-Asmodeus sends a legion to destroy them utterly
-But the Trinity uses an uber-quake to remove most of the devils (and switch some PCs around as usual)

Devils remaining after the quake:
-4x Barbed Devil
-2x Horned Devil
-1x Ice Devil

Through the doors is a large open chamber. Stone room, roughly square. Several alcoves around the room contain motifs and shrines to various gods.
-The far left alcove (far end of the room from the door) has stairs leading downward -- no mural, but words: "All things are possible."
-The far right alcove is Lolth's, and has a secret staircase from the Underdark coming up.

Gods can observe within 100' x DR of any worship area devoted to them. So all the gods can observe the Temple.

Memorials:
-Melora: Scenery drawing of a forest clearing, with words: "The Fey will always remember"
-Lolth: Figure shrouded in darkness with her back to the mural: "Love brings the fiercest betrayal of all"

There are murals/motifs here, portraits of the gods:
-Boccob: man in a gray robe, with a look of musing upon his face, stroking his chin thoughtfully, empty-handed
-Fharlanghn: old man with a long beard and a walking stick, weathered skin, hand over his brow, looking to the horizon, where the sun is setting
-Garl: golden hands, golden eyes, and a golden cape -- flamboyantly dressed, a mischievous grin on his face
-Heironeous & Hextor: back to back, but eying each other -- one white stone, one back stone -- white knight and black knight
-Kord: human-looking dude with tons of muscles cracking his knuckles, lightning storm above his head (possibly some flaming motorcycles)
-Olidammara: this mural has missing stonework in the shape of a man
-Pelor: as described when people met him
-Vecna: man in a black cloak, shadows hide his face, has a staff -- imagery does NOT indicate a missing eye or hand
-Wee Jas: small old woman, bent from age (withered?), carrying a scale, sightless eyes

Racial murals:
-Bane: fearsome goblin warrior, not muscular but lithe-looking dressed in light armor with a poisoned spear
-Corellon: fine-boned man with thin circlet, long blonde hair, raising the long sword in triumph
 --Sehanine: drawn in the same mural as Corellon, at his side -- silvery hair and eyes, very beautiful, moon behind her head, one of her hands is an animal claw
-Erythnul: massive oni clad in hides, red-skinned with horns
-Gruumsh: one-eyed orc with a enraged countenance
-Io: a multicolored metallic disk (some kind of shrine with both Bahamut and Tiamat symbols) has a jagged edge down the middle
-Ioun: placid-looking eladrin who seems to be staring out of the mural
-Moradin: regal-looking dwarf with hammer and crown, braided beard and broad grin
-Obad-Hai: intense-looking ageless man apparently made out of bark and leaves, with roots weaving through him like veins, similar to a genasi
-Yondalla: cheerful looking halfling woman with a sling, leaping

NOT PRESENT:
-Avandra: new god
-Bahamut, Tiamat: aspects of Io
-Ehlonna: new, took Melora's place
-Erathis: new god, formed from creation of the warforged and Delphian phenomenon
-Raven Queen: MYSTERY
-Nerull: MYSTERY
-St. Cuthbert: MYSTERY
-Kiaransalee, the Lady of Pain: NO
-Ghaunadaur, the One Who Waits: NO
-Asmodeus: new
-Demogorgon: new

Other words:
-Ringed in the holy symbols of all the gods: "This monument marks the first time that the gods come together as one. Let us hope it is not the last."

Down the spiral stairs opens into another chamber with a transparent floor of force, and a curved ceiling covered in a thousand points of light connected by glowing lines. Through the floor, a similar dome is visible.

At the center of the room is a tracing of light in the form of a rune: "Perspective"

Standing on the rune, a musical chime sounds (bards take note). The one standing there is able to move the map with a successful Concentration check.

After the party climbs the spiral staircase again, the doors open, and Vance stands in the doorway, his hands outstretched: "My son. I have been looking for you... EVERYWHERE."
