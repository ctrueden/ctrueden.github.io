F-09: Red

RED GROUP - Nov 11

Who wrote session notes for Red #7?

QUAKE:
-LOSE: Rebin, Sahrit
-GAIN: Isaline, Gwenvyth

PLAYERS: Allen, Elise, Jesse, Kelsey, Mark, Paul

Isaline in the Empire:
 - Van Wagram is a member of the necromancer guild, and a withered vampire
 - Van Wagram has a fair number of mooks that he has made into withered vampires as well
 - Van Wagram strikes to discredit her politically, and get her arrested
 - Most people in the Empire do not remember her
 - Corvidae is informed that he must help her (from visions from the Raven Queen)
 - Blackdale is informed (from Vance) that Isaline is to be destroyed

Red group helps defend the Emperor from the high priests, one of which is a doppelganger.

Marquises perspectives:
-Ironheart sides with the priests, because what the Emperor says can't possibly be true. He has brought "Righteous Fury," a Witheredbane greatsword. (His other sword is "Order's Wrath," which is Feybane.)
-Bloodletter sides against Ironheart, and believes that killing the Emperor's ghost would still be treason.
-Dragontamer sides with the party, because he's heard too much that fits too well with this ludicrous story.
-Van Wagram sides with whoever is going to win -- and so sides with the priests.

If Red group does poorly, Emperor is destroyed, but returns in 1d4 days.
-There should also be a possibility of the priests being able to permanently destroy the Emperor, if the party is stupid.
-If they do well, they avert this situation, and he goes into hiding or something.
-Two priests of "Bahamut" guarding the door, one of whom is the doppelganger -- he takes the form of one of the Emperor's
bodyguards: Tuan Hungurrr.

Back in Niflheim:

Since a doppelganger is killed, Lyra Dragontamer (the cripple's wife) invites Kith to join the Brethren, and the ritual commences (see Paul secrets page).

Give kith a choice to join the guild or not:
  * invited, ritual is applied, his mentality determines whether it works
  * if it works, he becomes a doppelganger, and he learns lots of secrets
  * if it fails, it is because his mentality allows the lady of pain to show up
      o the one who remembers has the sword of kas on him
        - mordollwen gave it to him, because it was Tiamat's will (she knows it will hurt them)
      o lady of pain grabs it with a chain, kills two of the five guildmasters: Underdark and Empire (Lyra)
      o the other three teleport away
      o the lady pulls the sword free from Lyra and presents it to Kith
      o when Kith takes it, there is a quake, and he appears back with the group
      o kith can sense that the sword has absorbed the two doppelgangers' essences
      o after the quake, it has become a tiny longsword

After the ritual, it is probable that they will still need another doppelganger. But Kith, being a hunter now, disqualifies Simone (too close to Kith).

Someone calls Kith "Child of death"... Corvidae?

The party must deal with the aftermath of Lyra's death.
