Dark – one week of prep time

Upon being teleported into Elyria, you have one week to prepare an assault into the keep of Marquis van Wagram.  From an ooc standpoint, this is being done to keep you in line with the other group, so that a joint session can occur.  From an IC standpoint, that's how long it takes to gather enough information, floor plans etc. to be comfortable making an assault in.

You were teleported in by Derros Rholle, and accompanied by Jadia Bocio, Kita Rosen's assistant and the individual who disintegrated Admatha's body.  Derros will communicate a need to assist his mistress in resolving matters so that war does not come unless your attempt fails.  He will teleport away, but be around briefly to answer questions within his ability to do so.

Jadia Bocio will remain behind and assert, with as few words as possible, that she has been tasked with ensuring your success.  As such, she will provide each of the three of you with 50,000 gold coins in platinum, which can be used to procure magical and mundane equipment within the city.  There are a few restrictions; most items requiring an Evil caster or similarly evil magic are not likely to be found.  Pretty much anything else is fair game.

Jadia is a skilled Rogue.  She can gather information, attempt to steal floor plans, or similar activities.  She can also provide assistance to anyone in the group attempting such tasks.  If necessary, she can also be called upon to assassinate a person viewed as important to a successful infiltration.

 

Haroun's vision

  • Strong sense of urgency, to act now, or suffer these events
