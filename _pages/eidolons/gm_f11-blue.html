Brotherhood of Order's first target was Tat'enda. Second targets were: Trimsaran, Intempestivus and Carthage, a Horde settlement where Kith and the Vanquisher were camping with their armed force some time after Warder's Stand.

Next target is Heimdall, in the north of the Empire. Kith and the Vanquisher are able to get this information out of the cultist, who is wearing a Guild ring that the Vanquisher cannot see.

Once the party is all together, they travel to Heimdall to halt the next Ordering attempt. Berserkers and cultists and terror, oh my!

As the nexus returns to equilibrium, there is a quake that causes Biger and Ed to disappear. In their place appear Cameron (or whatever Jason's character's name is at the moment) and Isaline.
