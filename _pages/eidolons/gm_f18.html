Beardthicket's move

  • He makes a play at one of the two remaining Bael'turath-accessible nexuses
    • Third one collapsed months ago, supposedly naturally (but in reality Beardthicket did it very stealthily)
  • Beardthicket still doesn't know about the Emperor (and Bahamut's champions) directly aiding him
  • Warlocks are distracted with their squabble, so he attacks
  • Warlock loyalists need to protect the nexus being targeted
  • Beardthicket collapses the tunnels with an earthquake
  • Fiendgaze (legitimately loyal to Asmodeus) leaves to protect the nexus, and digs with vitriolic blast
  • Small contingent of loyalists remain to guard the palace
  • Baalzebul & the rogue warlocks attack while the barricade is weak
  • Dardanos takes Bahamut's Champions via divine intervention to hit the loyalist warlocks from behind in the tunnels

 

Beardthicket's knowledge

  • Bahamut wants to collapse all the Imperial nexuses, to purge Asmodeus's followers
  • Doesn't care about collapsing them outside the Empire
  • If shown that nexuses can be restored, will be impressed, but still concerned that they could be re-inverted
  • If told about the nexus in balance, will want to see it
  • If told about Moradin's fate, will want to help rescue him
  • If threatened too much, will try to flee
  • Sora shares most of his sensibilities, but cares even more about Bahamut's will, whereas Beardthicket cares about carrying out the Emperor's wishes (and gets a perverse sense of joy out of being difficult)
