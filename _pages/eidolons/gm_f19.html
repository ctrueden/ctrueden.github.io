The Gods

Most gods (including, for somewhat complex reasons, both Vance/Vecna and Boccob/Vecna) are busy shoring up Tharizdun's prison, because the Ordering is weakening it. However, some gods do not need to participate in that, and have an interest in the Oraculum situation:

  • Ioun - busy during the talks but sent the Sending to get things started
  • Ehlonna - present, as the Fey Court representative
  • Kas - shows up for the meeting representing St. Cuthbert's interests
  • Kord - blows off his responsibility to take part in the battle beneath Factum; leaves afterwards
    • arrives the night before with ehlonna and avandra
    • when the hordes attack, pulls twin greatswords and jumps down through the rift
  • Avandra - rallies the free cities to the cause!
    • rivalry with kord
    • wears leather, carries bolas, fights with spear & net
    • rides a magnificent stallion
    • tall, athletic-looking, tan
    • confident and opinionated
  • Erathis - flying Factum (& Sollicitus)
    • mechanical arm, leg, ear, eye, all same side
    • lined scars down one cheek, metal underneath (like circuitry?)
    • when the arm, leg or eye move, there are little noises
      • eye doesn't have to sync up with other eye
      • arm and leg can bend in any direction, at various points; both are prehensile
    • intense optimism, certainty that mortalkind can overcome any obstacle
    • could come off as a bit a zealot, because her gaze is intense and maybe a bit creepy sometimes
  • Erythnul + Gruumsh + Bane
    • the three agree (which is rare) that Oraculum shouldn't be allowed to spread
    • so they tell their followers to raid Factum
    • they limitedly support Demogorgon in its pursuit of conflict
