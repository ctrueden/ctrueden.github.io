F-22: Blue

-Guild plotline.
-Battle between Calanon, Mammon and the Guild.
-Kith finds out about what will happen by 'happening upon' a shrine to Wee Jas.  There is a message there.  "Tatenda will be your last chance.  Go now or she will be lost forever."
-Upon arrival, there is a heated battle between Calanon, the Devils, and the Guild members (fifteen dopplegangers in ironwood equipment).
-In stage one, Calanon is driven off by the Dopplegangers and Devils.
-In stage two, the dopplegangers are in conflict with the Devils.  Mammon seizes the sword from the Guild leader.
-In stage three, the dopplegangers are subdued by the Devils and are subsequently killed by Mammon with the sword.
-During this time, the party can watch from nearby and be stealthy.
