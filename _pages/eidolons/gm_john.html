Character Secrets - Eidylol Pes

GM discussion of John's character to go here. 

One basic idea with warforged is that they usually stick to doing what they were made to do. They may be really smart, but still they have a "nature." Ed's nature is to be adaptive, which was something that had been tried before, but not met with this level of success. Why not?

Ed was forged by Moradin a long time ago. Garl is insanely jealous of the design, but has not been able to replicate it.

Theta, trained as a ninja, gets infected, because warforged are not immune to the plague. Hence, Theta becomes a robot ninja zombie.
