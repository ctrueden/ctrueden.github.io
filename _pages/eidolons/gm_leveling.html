Leveling

Chapter 1: 7-10 (gain a level approx. every third session)
Chapter 2: 12-15 (gain a level approx. every third session)
Chapter 3: 17-21 (gain a level every session except the last)

If someone takes notes or performs other useful services, he gets his last level each chapter a session early.

Experience awarded according to Fast progression of the Pathfinder character advancement table; see this spreadsheet for more details.

For starting gold, see Table: Character Wealth By Level.
