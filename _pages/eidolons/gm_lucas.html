Character Secrets - Kumo

Lucas's character is one of Olidammara's copies who joined the Guild of the Invisible Hand. The process acted strangely on someone with divine essence, and the subsequent reaction converted him into a being of total formlessness. He soon forgot his past. Left with only the impulse to reproduce, he has proliferated himself into a totally new race of beings.

Does the Guild know of Kumo and the other kitsune? We have indicated no, since Jason doesn't know what he is. But he could find out—the five Guildmasters certainly remember the Incident of the Failed Joining or whatnot—just haven't connected it to Kumo yet.
