Character Secrets - Joseph Vernor

Joe is a shadar kai, from a village on the plane of shadow. Some shadar kai retain their shadow traits when sent forth into the multiverse, and some do not (Raven Queen's choice?).

 

Jessica

Jessica Reynolds, Joe's girlfriend, is a St. Cuthbert avatar! It makes sense; she's attracted to humans, and meddles to involve them in the plot to end Tharizdun's suffering. She is "the only one like him, with magic."

When she first kissed him, it was to send him forth into the world.

 

Corvidae

Also, Joe would be familiar with a young deva (Isaline's Corvidae in his past life) who appeared in the village about a year before Joe disappeared.

 

Runes

Joe has an extra runemage tattoo—a raven glyph

Also, Joe's runes are inscribed with ink including iron as a component

  • if he quick casts too many runes, he starts to become incorporeal
  • if he quick casts them all, he disappears and wakes up later in the wastes again
