Character Secrets - Sahrit Doubain

Paimon

Vance (Sahrit's father) found out about nexus hearts because he and Paimon were on the team that implanted a nexus heart into young Haroun. It was only then that he traveled to the nomads, to gain their trust and learn more about the properties of the nexus hearts. (In one version of history, Paimon never met Vance and stayed behind to work with Haroun further.)

 

Entropomancer

If evil Sahrit manifests shards of entropy too often, what happens will be erratic, but could include:

  • Shard begins to grow at a rate of 5ft per round, until it envelops an extremely large area.
  • Shard begins to chase Sahrit around.
  • Shard explodes.
  • Shard's inexorable pull phenomenon becomes exponentially stronger.
  • Shard withers Sahrit at an extremely rapid rate.

 

Brand of Vance

Evil Sahrit paid 35mp for this artifact. It works on a corruption system: when he crosses a threshold, he knows he has done something to himself that he can't undo. He is learning to tap into the artifact to draw on its power in various ways:

  • [known] gain a corrupt action point; stacks with "normal" action points (costs corruption equal to the die roll addition)
  • gain a corrupt point of essence (1 corruption)
  • divine intervention (costs corruption equal to the level of the spell if his character level is high enough; or twice that if not)
  • [known] switch between good and evil versions of himself (5 corruption); can be done as an immediate reaction, and his other self is at full vitality
  • [known] switch between red and blue group, swapping with one other person (his choice if specified, otherwise random or GM choice)

The corruption mechanic is as follows:

  • every 5 points of corruption, he notices a significant change in the hand as it burrows toward his heart
  • every 10 points of corruption, his Con permanently drops by 1
  • Good and Evil share a corruption pool, but only Evil can tap into it (Good's only available ability is to relinquish control to Evil)
  • when his natural Con (excluding item bonuses) becomes 0, he becomes an NPC under Vance's control
