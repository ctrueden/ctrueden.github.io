Ryan / Rebin

  • x

 

Fochlucan's Erosion

  • asmodeus is the first geomancer
  • she can "erode" or "corrupt" history, cherry picking results favorable to her and slowly propagating them by rigging which worlds "split" during a quake
  • party visits fochlucan, with a repaired nexus
  • the next morning, fochlucan is abandoned and there is a destroyed nexus
  • but there was no quake
  • simone can perceive that the probability of certain historical events is actually changing, long after the fact ("aftershock")
  • the night before, they ate dinner and talked to people
  • but the next morning, they are hungry and can't clearly remember the conversations
  • this form of "cheatery" that Asmodeus is doing is normally perfect, but the party's growing link with Kiaransalee allows them to sense that something is wrong, and ultimately as they grow
  • more powerful, to determine more precisely what has changed
  • describe to simone that it is an "erosion of history" -- most unnatural feeling

 

Melora's Death

  • Boccob is the one primarily responsible for Melora's death?
  • First, Boccob uses a ritual to steal his eidolon's power (i.e., Vecna)
  • Then, secrets become part of his portfolio, and he begins to blackmail the Lady of Pain
  • When killing Melora, Boccob claimed to be speaking on behalf of the gods, but in actuality, was lying about that
  • After the temple's construction, but before the murals were added, he arranged her death
  • He wanted to steal her power, to regain some of that he lost
  • He knew about the Lady of Pain's "secret sanity" and blackmailed her to do his bidding
  • Unfortunately, unlike with Vecna, Boccob was unable to claim Melora's power, because it scattered
  • He suspects the Lady of Pain had something to do with that, but can't prove it
