S-02

Session outline

  1. Extended recap of the situation
  2. Let them coordinate the assault: work with the five chieftains (see below)
    • Vanquisher is clearly in charge of the Hordes
    • The chieftains are aware there will be an elite team of people who will be assisting them
    • They also understand that the team will be taking a sword, but all other spoils are up for grabs
    • Cameron is to make sure everything goes smoothly, but not explicitly in charge
    • No Guildmasters are directly involved (better to keep cover in the long term)
  3. Mass combat battle!
  4. Escort Sword to Eloyes as quickly as possible (phantom steeds summoned by one of the chieftains)
    • Gnome necromancer attacks them while they are escorting the sword in the sky

Next session: Cameron hires Kith and gives him the Sword, and the Dark group gets involved in the city sacking

 

Horde units

1) Warriors of the Flayed Skull [ogres]

  • Chieftain: The Vanquisher, M ogre mage disguised as a human -- geomancer 11, fairly unstoppable :-)
    • Wears a fearsome mask
    • Soft-spoken, but when he speaks everyone else listens, and shuts up
    • Is actually Boris, corrupted "tiefling" geomancer
    • Master geomancer spell: jump into a ley line (obviously corrupting it temporarily) to return to any nexus (corrupted or not)
    • Uses this tactic to maintain both personas, about 50/50
  • elite ogre warriors
  • Tokens: 10 (~60 5th level warriors)

2) Shattered Arrow [mixed orcs, ogres and crossbreeds]

  • Chieftain: Bolnarin, M half-orc, half-ogre barbarian -- all ugly
    • Boisterous and disagreeable
    • Insists his people go in first
  • warriors are very ugly, very big
  • warriors can deflect arrows (and cannon?) with their mighty chests
  • Tokens: 8 (~80 3rd level warriors)

3) Wind Tamers [goblins]

  • Chieftain: Atria Velan, F goblin wind shaman
    • Soft-spoken and scarily intense
    • Mainly listens, but volunteers her tribe's expertise
  • harness the power of sandstorms
  • archers with no attack penalty or miss chance from the winds
  • and the shamans can fly
  • Tokens: 2 (~12 5th level shamans)

4) Bloody Thunder [orcs]

  • Chieftain: Grogalik, M orc savage bard
    • Boisterous and a braggart
    • Argues with Bolnarin
  • martial/arcane mix with sonic and lightning damage
  • shamans working together can cause earthquakes that affect the terrain (possibly could stop the cannons by tipping the lineship)
  • Tokens: 6 (~90 2nd level warriors)

5) Blackspear tribe [mixed orcs, hobgoblins and goblins]

  • General: Grugar, M orc rogue who fancies himself a king
    • Wears finery that doesn't quite fit, and lots of jewelry, some of which is women's jewelry
    • Arrogant, but not stupid
    • Does not volunteer to charge in first
    • Will "hit them from behind"
  • Grugar is always teamed with a group of thugs, "Grugar's Boyz," who do the actual fighting for him
  • Roguier tribe; favors sneak attack; any downed enemy is coup de graced
  • Most numerous â€“ "cannon fodder"
  • Tokens: 4 (120 1st level warriors)

 

Delphia units

1) Lineship unit

  • General: Captain Banthiel, M eladrin lineship captain, bladesinger with longsword; level 10 (fighter 5/runemage 1/bladesinger 4)
  • Lieutenant: Thustifer Pennyweg, M gnome engineer, "trapsmith"; level 5
    • throws some ornithopters (mechanical bats), then runs into the hold, slams the door and seals it with arcane lock
    • Once the group busts in there, they take some fire damage from a trap he put on the door, then they can punk him
  • Hero: Gwen
  • Hero: Rebin
  • Hero: Sahrit
  • Tokens: 5 (around 50 3rd level defenders, largely undead)

2) Outpost unit

  • Captain: Sasha Skintearer, mercenary captain (Za'har & Arwel's group): female gnoll cleric of Kord; level 8 (gnoll 1, cleric 8)
  • Hero: Arwel
  • Hero: Haroun
  • Hero: Za'har
  • Tokens: 15 (nearly 500 1st level soldiers)

 

Events

Round 0:

  • Sandstorm begins, obscuring visibility
  • extra 2d6 damage whenever damage is rolled
  • whenever a Delphia units take chip damage, they lose an extra chip

Round 1:

  • Lineship cannon hits the Shattered Arrow, knocks down the warriors... and they get back up

Round 2:

  • Bloody Thunder shamans cause an earthquake around the lineship, which tilts it and negates the cannons
