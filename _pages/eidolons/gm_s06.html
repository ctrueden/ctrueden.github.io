Aftermath

1. Iraja is in the room. Adamatha enters. Admatha does her thing, absorbing divine essence from the sword; sword disintegrates.

2. Za'har Kool-Aids in through the window. Yos presumably enters as well. Kumo grants Za'har's wish... (see below)

3. Naxk is following Vance, and sees he is approaching the building. Naxk observes the dragonborn patrolling the streets all fall unconscious. Vance pauses and look pained, and then order his minions to "return to the Wood."

4. Vance proceeds into the building past the incapacitated dragonborn. up the stairs and into the room. Asks Admatha: "What have you done?"

5. Vance tells them about his reason for seeking the sword: stop it from being used to destroy the world. (He tells them stuff in line with Iraja's first doomsday scenario involving the Empire.)

6. He explains that there is a remaining threat: a name named Sahrit Doubain, who Vance warns them about: "he bears the mark of the sword and he must be stopped from using it at the place of power."

7. During this discussion, Pelor also shows up. If the Yoses are still asleep, they wake up due to Pelor's presence. Time stops when Pelor appears, but it doesn't affect Vance. Pelor and Vance are both momentarily surprised to see the other.

Pelor apologizes to Za'har for putting him in an impossible situation. "It seems even the power of the gods cannot change fate. However, while your actions have not preserved the life of the Emperor, what has transpired here has changed what is to come. Thank you for your service; the gods remember their debts." Pelor responds to questions, but takes his leave promptly after that, saying: "Henceforth, your names will be known to my followers."

8. Vance indicates to Naxk that he may be able to escape from his contract (see below).

9. Vance offers them an escort out of town, so there will be some undead protection, but there will be fighting versus the orcs.
