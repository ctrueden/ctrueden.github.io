Plotline: The Plague

Migration:

  • REMAIN – Biger, Ed, Isaline, Joesph
  • PLUS – Vashir
  • MINUS – Iraja, Kumo

Location: Oraculum, hiding from demons in a stable

A demon outside notices the party looking through the window, screams out a cry, a bunch of other demons answer, and they all lunge toward the stable doors, breaking them down... and there is a quake. The door is back, the demons are gone, Iraja and Kumo are gone, and Vashir is there (Perception check DC 40 to see receding chains into a closing rift). Vashir remembers melting the egg, then being grabbed by chains and dragged into a rift.

  • Why are demons eating corpses? They've been infected with the Plague.
  • Graz'zt has demons killing demons to deal with it, but only elite patrols remain unaffected.

Character stuff:

  • Vashir: Wanted in Delphia for escaping from his brainwashing punishment at Arx.
  • Ed: Wanted by Zan & Mo because they are jerks.
    • Theta may find him here.
    • But Myl os Val is also here, fighting demons, to help Ed. She views the flow as a tapestry she is helping to weave. That's why she trained him before, to "weave the future," and she's doing it again now.
  • Biger: Wanted back by his former owner, Thruslik Ralomer (a droll, money-driven gnome).
  • Isaline: Graz'zt wants to take her hostage, as a chip to use with the Empire.
  • Joesph: Wanted secretly by the noble families of Arx, and publically for vandalizing the Arx library.

Hence, the constable of Arx (of all Delphian cities, actually)—Lemeth Hawn, a gray elf Constable spellthief 10/shadowdancer 2/mindbender 4, detect thoughts at will—is actively seeking them all. Graz'zt put a bounty on all these people, and the constable is pursuing that, while also dealing with the Plague outbreak.

They see a wanted poster in the stable: "Do not rent or sell a horse to any of these people..."

Graz'zt has charmed Ararir Carmik (the now-full-grown elf girl who nursed Kith back to health ~100 years ago) and made her his slave, to provoke Kith into challenging him directly. He keeps a constant watch on her, waiting for Kith to make a move.
