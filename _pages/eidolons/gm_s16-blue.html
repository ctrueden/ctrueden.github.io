Why is Io in two halves?

He led the ritual, and hence has a residual link to Tharizdun. As the chained god's madness grew, Io became infected by it (within a few hundred years). To protect himself from this insanity, he created the two dragon orbs to bind the poles, splitting his personality. He entrusted the orbs to his two most loyal high priests, knowing that over time his neutrality would become skewed on the mortal realm.
