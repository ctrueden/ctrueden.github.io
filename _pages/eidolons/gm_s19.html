Representatives

Meeting area is divided into a "dart board" style layout. In the center is a round table with one leader per faction. Behind each leader is a second ring where each leader's advisors are seated. Additional outer rings seat the other invited interested parties of each faction.

Clockwise:

  • Delphia
    • 1—Erathis, Of Technology and Magic, the Inspiration, Agent of Civilization
      • mechanical arm, leg, ear, eye, all same side
      • lined scars down one cheek, metal underneath (like circuitry?)
      • when the arm, leg or eye move, there are little noises
        • eye doesn't have to sync up with other eye
        • arm and leg can bend in any direction, at various points; both are prehensile
      • intense optimism, certainty that mortalkind can overcome any obstacle
      • could come off as a bit a zealot, because her gaze is intense and maybe a bit creepy sometimes
    • 2—

      • Minerva, plenipotentiary of Solicitus – female gnome – bookish but very wise and careful

        • can drop the walls at Oraculum & turn the defenses against the demons
      • Sidolphus, plenipotentiary of Terminas – female elf – no nonsense, get this done
        • Simone sees that she met with Demogorgon
        • wearing a stolen ring (Demogorgon acquired two in the sacking of Oraculum) under gloves

    • describe the state of the other Delphian cities
    • ask various leaders to give status reports on their situations
