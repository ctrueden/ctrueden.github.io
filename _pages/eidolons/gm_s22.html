S-22

  • View
  • Edit
  • Outline
  • Grant

Players: Andy, Alex, Jeff, Dave, Joel, Mark, Moe

 

Events

  1. Near Intempestivus (within 1 hex)
  2. Find increasing numbers of corpses, rent apart, sometimes surgical kills; looks like the people were running from something, terrified
  3. Their contingent begins to disappear as the quakes increase in frequency
  4. If they allow themselves to disappear, they find themselves in the prison
  5. Otherwise, if they reach the barrier, they see it moving outward
  6. After entering the Ordered area (a 100-mile radius, for the geographically minded), they find Intempestivus and Dispater's tower at the center
  7. Inside the city, some of the terrified people running were turned to metal
  8. The tower has devil guards (two pit fiends: fight, sneak or bribe)

In the tower:

  • Main hall has iron statues, lush red carpet, tapestries, beautiful ironwork fountains, paintings, other art items on display.
  • Iron statues on display in two rows of four: Gwenvyth, echidnox, platypod, lammasu; Vhaeraun, unknown dragonborn, solar, unicorn.
  • The prisoners are in a technomagical chamber: glass tubes filled with liquid, unconscious, tubes plugged in, being transformed into devils. Gear is all there, except for Moradin's hammer, which Dispater has now.
    • 4 nomad leaders (90% each; and 4 more have already been transformed)
    • Sasha of Tane'klenya (100%)
    • Rebin (100%)
    • Talori, geomancer of Arx (50%)
    • Tallahassee Wizzlesnaps (30%)
    • Iraja (10%)
    • Balanced energy could undo the damage, but it takes time
  • Iron golem, Talos, is the tower's guardian, at the end of the main hall
  • Two spiral staircases at the rear left & right lead up to the next level, the audience chamber (Dispater's throne room)
  • Four doors left and right (two doors each side) leading into the bowels of the tower
  • Gravity at different orientations, difficult to climb the tower

 

Dispater

  • Wants to meet with the party after their arrival, to discuss their intentions; sends servant Desmond (a bearded devil) to escort them
  • Wants to talk them into going to the 9th layer of hell, to deliver them to Asmodeus
  • Willing to bargain, and would show them the chamber with their friends (even release them) in exchange for their cooperation
  • Hates Baalzebul, loves Asmodeus
  • Dispater attempts to trick the party into going to Hell to recover the other dragon orb
  • In reality, he is leading them into a trap (Bluff +64), delivering them into Asmodeus's hands
  • If they go for it, this leads to Prime through a portal in the 9th hell
  • If not, Dispater "regrets we could not reach an understanding" and has Desmond "escort them out"
    • On their way out, Desmond apologizes, "I'm terribly sorry, my lords" and teleports away, leaving them to battle Talos
    • In addition, they can hear from Calanon about Mammon later

 

Battle with Talos

  • Ground is unhallowed (+2 to AC and saves for Talos)â€”Unhallow effect prevents teleportation (dimensional anchor)
  • Doors are arcane locked (DC 50), and covered with walls of force (340 hp each, hardness 30)
  • Water level in fountains rises to fill the room in one round
    • rising water in round one counts as difficult terrain
    • after first round, must fight underwater: DC 15 swim check after the first round to move
  • At the tip of each stair's bannister, two ornate wrought iron "turrets" begin to glow, and each use greater dispel magic on an intelligently chosen enemy each round (dispel check 40, up to 5 spells)
