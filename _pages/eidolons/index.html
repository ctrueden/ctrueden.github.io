Eidolons

Welcome to the Eidolons campaign web site! The campaign used Pathfinder with modifications. It ran from July 2009 until August 2010, and was divided into three phases (plot arcs).

Number of players: At various times there were as many 18 active players, with varying parties each time. Jon and I (the co-GMs) often ran two simultaneous groups in two adjacent rooms. Due to the metaphysics of the setting, characters would sometimes even switch groups mid-session.
