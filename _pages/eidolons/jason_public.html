-Wanted-

Cameron Paige

For multiple counts of theft, petty theft, and burglary

A substantial reward is offered for information leading the capture of one Cameron Paige and the return of valuable artifacts stolen from several homes and museums in the last two months.  It is believed that she may go by several other aliases, although none are known.

She appears elven but her short stature leads us to believe that she is also part halfling.  She has short unkempt mousy brown hair and a generally unremarkable appearance other than her bright green eyes.  She appears quite androgynous and tends to blend in easily with a crowd.  She was last seen in dull brown fairly plain common street clothes.  In at least one instance she was seen dressed very convincingly as a male so be aware that she may be well disguised.

Anyone encountering her should consider her moderately dangerous.  She incapacitated several guards in at least one robbery.  She also had information related to some of these artifacts known to very few.  It is believed that she is skilled at extracting information.  Be advised to avoid speaking to her entirely until she is safely in custody.
