 Za'har is an often soft-spoken deva, obviously hailing from the empire. If asked, he will indicate that he does not refresh, nor will he approach too close to a nexus. He possesses an array of semi-mystic fighting techniques, and clearly benefits from imperial training. His reasons for traveleing abroad are not immediately apparant.
