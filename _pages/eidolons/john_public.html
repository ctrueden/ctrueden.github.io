Eidylol Pes (approaching drums [Elven] ) aka experiment #357001-Beta aka Ed, a simply built humanoid model warforged no taller then 5 and a half feet.  Its hull is a dull white, but smooth and devoid of any dents whatsoever.  Its face is generic and may as well be featureless, a dull slope implying the existence of a nose.  What's more, its hands can't seem to stop twitching.

However its eyes, dark orbs of black with the light glinting off their smooth surface, they almost seem to look into you, like its looking at your insides.  

Ed typically wears a large brown cloak to conceal his features, a hood up to cover his head.  He doesn't speak often, but if you can ignore the problem with the sync, its generally worth your while to listen.
