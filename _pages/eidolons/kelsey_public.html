Those who actually tap the power of the nexuses to peer into the fathomless depths of time and pull prophecy from the mist are a rare and protected sect of the technomagical society.  They are kept segregated from the rest of the rest of the population, so the pressures of the masses do not pollute their words.  Their techniques are hidden, their knowledge carefully doled out to those who need to know.

Haroun appears as a genasi with earth and water influences, wearing a voluminous and covering robe.  From under the hood, his eyes glow lightly, a common side effect of overexposure to the energies of a nexus.  His face is unlined, as though he has never withered a day in his life, yet he occasionally breaks into a terrible hacking cough, and he walks with a limp.

He walks outside the cities of the technomagical prophets, and he is hunted.  Although he is naive and unaccustomed to the world he finds himself in, he knows things he should not possibly be able to know, and this knowledge protects him.  He wields geomantic energies and stranger powers with skill and familiarity, and does not speak of the past.
