Chapter 1 Epilogue - Kumo

Once Yos went to Zahar, Zahar warned him off of Vashir, because Vashir and Dragontamer were in cahoots.  Instead, he spoke highly of Ironheart's (in the south) dedication to justice and the preservation of the Empire.  So, Yos went down there to approach him.  He's founded a small (possibly very small - think like 5-10 people) chapter somewhere very out of the way.  He and Ironheart are now working together, in a resistance against the Regent.  Of course, Ironheart can't openly embrace Yos and his revolution, but he is helping him behind the scenes, in the hopes that someday he will be in a position to help Yos openly.  Of course, this means that certain powerful people in the South of the Empire will now know that Kumo is a murderous, shapeshifting assassin part.
