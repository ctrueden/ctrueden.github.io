Private Details - Kumo

[Kumo's character sheet]

Race: Kitsune

The Kitsune are based on the trickster/fox people of Japanese Mythology.  Kumo will find a person or group that he intends to try and join.  He will shadow them for a short time in the form of a fox, attempting to learn some information about what they are likely to accept and appreciate.  He will then approach the person/group in a personality he feels they are likely to accept.

Kumo then tries to learn about the person and become close to them.  He tries to find out their deepest wish, what they want more than anything else.  He does this through traditional means - simply talking and getting to know them.  There is no supernatural or psionic insight in this process, and this means that the intentions can become easily muddled, and the process is extremely fallible.

Once Kumo has decided on a wish to grant, he will initiate a quake.  In this quake, he will align to a reality in which the wish has been granted.  At the same time, he will create a copy of himself.  This copy will start with the appearance of the character that Kumo was playing when the quake was created, and will have a personality influenced by the emotional state of the person whose wish he is granting.  (See the list of "children" below for examples of this.)

When he's alone, Kumo tends to take the form of a dirty and bedraggle man, with skin tanned and leathered by the sun and wind, or a fox.  However, as a Kitsune, the concept of gender does not apply in the traditional sense.

  • Geomancer, not gestalt
  • Using Chameleon prestige class (RoD 112) starting at level 7  (will have five levels of geomancer before that)
  • Skills will be Bluff, Disguise, Diplomacy, Geomancy, Sense Motive
  • Trigger a quake when kinship is strong enough
  • This quake grants their secret? wish and creates a duplicate of Kumo
  • The original Kumo, his work done, leaves, and begins to forget his past
  • Kumo's copy hangs out with the group for a while, retaining his manufactured personality, but eventually gets an "itch" to leave
  • Then he leaves, reverts to his generic "I must breed" status, and forms a new false persona. He often (but not always) rejoins the same group as before, as the new persona
  • When no one is around, he looks kind of like a "dirty hippie"

 

NPCs

"Children of Kumo" - When Kumo breeds, he creates a copy that is loosely based on him, but is influenced by the circumstances of the "birth".  It takes the form of whatever Kumo was at the time the quake was initiated, although obviously, being Kitsune, this form is easily malleable.  Its personality is heavily influenced by the emotional state of the person for whom the wish is granted.

Kasumi  (Spring Haze): Kasumi was born from the confusion of a farmer.  The farmer had inherited his family farm and had been working it his entire adult life.  As a child, it was impressed on him both how much an honor the inheritance was, as well as how much of a responsibility he had to his family to keep the farm running.  The farmer felt trapped in this role, unfulfilled, but subconsciously.  It wasn't until Kumo granted his wish to be free of his farm that he realized how much he yearned to be free of it.

Kasumi was created at this point, and due to the farmer's confusion and lack of self-reflection, Kasumi does not know who he is or what he wants.  He is ignorant of his powers, using them only in instinctual self-defense.  When he was initially "born", he was dressed as a well-to-do provincial merchant, in clothing that was simple but well-made.  However, since then Kasumi has simply been wandering, living by foraging or begging, and his clothes have turned to rags.  Appearing always as a male elf except in rare cases where he manages to change form, Kasumi constantly wears a look of shock and incomprehension.  Because he does not realize he was "born" from Kumo, he simply thinks he has amnesia, and has forgotten his past.  Most people think him mad.

 

Tora (Tiger):  Tora was born from a Geomancer, who had previously trusted and liked Kumo.  When Kumo initiated his "wish quake," the Geomancer was able to "read" it and realized what was happening.  His last feelings were those of anger, hatred, betrayal, and a strong desire to kill Kumo.

Thus, Tora endlessly hunts Kitsune in general and those of Kumo's line in particular.  He is totally, absolutely committed to this impossible task; all things exist as a means to this end.  He is aware of and in control of his powers, but generally eschews illusion, subterfuge and any manner of subtlety in favor of good old-fashioned violence.  He tends to favor the Nomads due to their freedom of movement.  (Kitsune, in general, avoid the Empire.  The inability to quake is existentially distasteful to them.)  Tora is a 3 point enemy.

 

Kaeribana (Flowers blooming out of season): Kaeribana was "born" from a young woman who desperately wished for true love.  Kaeribana was thus created from the wish, both the offspring of Kumo and the fulfillment of the wish itself, as the young woman's perfect mate.  Sadly, the Kitsune are a long-lived and very robust species, and he outlived his intended mate.

He wanders now, as Kitsune are wont to do.  He only remembers vague bits about the young woman, mostly remember from the beginning of her life rather than the end, because that was the reason for his being.  He has not met Kumo since his birth, but if he does, he will be passionately in love with Kumo, mirroring the love of the young woman.

 

Shiki and Shinkumo Nodoka (Shiki - Four Season Shinkumo - From Shin, meaning true or reborn, and Kumo, the character  Nodoka - Tranquil):  Shiki met Kumo and immediately knew who he was, accepting him and his nature.  Shiki and Kumo had a brief romantic encounter, in which she was supportive, understanding, and caring, seeming to love Kumo unconditionally.   She desired nothing but the happiness and fulfillment of Kumo, through their relationship.  From this wish Shinkumo was born, and the two have paired up, living together.

Shiki has a great deal of knowledge about the existence and lore of the Kitsune, in actuality being a very old Kitsune herself, much older than Kumo.  As Shiki and Shinkumo are both Kitsune in full knowledge and control of their abilities, gender and appearance are fluid with them.  Long-term pairing is rare with the Kitsune, but at least for now, Shiki and Shinkumo are very content to be together.

 

Non-Children:  

Yaremka: A male tiefling shopkeeper in the Underdark.  Kumo visits him often when in the guise of a merchant, in the guise of an attractive female tiefling.  Initially, this gave Kumo a mild advantage in commerce, and Kumo has kept the form to provide continuity of character.  Although Kumo has not tried to breed with Yaremka, Yaremka is aware that there's something going on with Kumo.  This is because Kumo makes very random business decisions, generally buying stuff to fuel illusions (and using the shop as a place to refill the Useful Pouch) rather than based on actual value.  Additionally, some of Kumo's stuff has proven to be illusory in the past.  However, since they have something of a friendship now, Yaremka has been able to cheat Kumo on a few occasions do to its very odd buying habits, and the fact that Kumo has never done anything overly destructive, Yaremka tolerates this and looks forward to seeing spider.  Yaremka assumes that Kumo is basically a traveling shyster, moving to keep ahead of his last victim.

Lucas: "Oh, we're going to the same place!"
Victim: "Yes we are!"
