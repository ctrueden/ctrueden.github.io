A human in a world devoid of his race, with memories not his own, some might notice that his race is only the start of the strange things about him.

He wears a travelers cloak over his simple robes, and is strangely pale.  He has black hair and grey eyes, and oddly shadows seem to cling to him even during mid-day.  However, despite his odd appearance, he is a friendly person.  While he is not a greatly accomplished Runemage, few have heard of his deeds, he has spent a number of years working as an escort for caravans in the wastes.   He tends to keep a low profile, and does not often talk of his past.  But in the wastes this is nothing strange, certainly not stranger then his species.

For those that have seen Anne she appears to be a 5-6 year old elven girl, with very long(waist-length) hair.  The hair is stark white, though its hard to tell through a ghosts normal ghostlieness.  Her hair covers her cute face, but its likely she can alter her appearance a small amount.  She also often appears to be wearing baggy clothes, which when a closer look is attempted seem fairly dirty.  She also appears wearing a oversized dress, all balled up at her feet, but this only seems to happen when she is in a good mood.
