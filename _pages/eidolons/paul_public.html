Public Profile - Kithlandil

Paul

Kithlandil

Male
Alignment:

Kithlandil is a very small person. Top half is humanoid, bottom half is grasshopper. He is faded, not vibrant like many fey. He rarely wears anything but a wee black cloak, except for a backpack attached to his hindquarters. He kills babies.
