Durus geomancers

Three geomancers working for the empire

  • one is corrupted (Boris), and is the one who actually corrupts the nexuses that are captured. he is an agent of the underdark. a tiefling.
  • other two are normal (Rory, a dwarf, high geomancer, and Sora, an illicit apprentice)
  • the emperor knows, the inner council knows, that they have an alliance with a race underground that dismantles the nexuses
  • Rory Beardthicket learns of the fochlucan incident and subsequent collapse, and manages to duplicate the effect on a corrupt nexus within the empire
  • the high geomancer didn't know about the corruption at the beginning, but one he learns, attempts to initiate an immediate collapse
  • this is what brings the underdark culture to the surface, into the war

 

Boris - corrupt geomancer

  • male tiefling, generally disguised as as elf
  • lawful evil
  • in charge of corrupting nexuses
  • has proven his loyalty over the years by actually destroying nexuses over time
  • emperor does not fully trust him, but close enough

 

Rory Beardthicket - high geomancer believes he is a good person

  • chaotic good
  • male dwarf
  • believes that the underdark races are evil
  • does not like alliance with underdark races
  • does not like Boris, reliant upon this one of questionable loyalty
  • wants to find a way to destroy the nexuses without the help of the corrupt geomancer
  • sends Tasha to fochlucan college to study the newly corrupted nexus, to do research
  • they say they found a way to undo it -- he said "of course I'll help you"
  • is not supposed to have taken on any apprentices; focused Tasha on hiding her power to avoid notice

 


Sora - third geomancer

  • female dragonborn
  • apprentice of approximate power to high geomancer (also luminary)
  • specialty is in hiding her power; appears as lower level than she is
  • close to Rory, shares confidence
  • also doesn't like Boris
  • was sent to study fochlucan nexus; helped in ritual
  • barely survived the collapse, and was not sucked in
  • because she was more powerful than the others, only she avoided pwnage (passed her save)
  • thus went back to Rory to report on the procedure and its results
  • now, they know how to forcibly collapse a corrupted nexus (but still not how to corrupt it)
  • are seeking someone who was at the incident; i.e., Nate
