Kiaransalee, the Lady of Pain

She is eight or nine feet tall, wrapped in black chains, and covered in black & silver metal, including her face which is covered by a mask. She has raven black hair. No hint of skin is visible, and the mask's expression is impassive and unchanging. She exudes an aura of inevitable death, with all the terror and authority that that feeling includes.

When she kills Melora, the chains become like blades, tearing her enemy asunder effortlessly.
