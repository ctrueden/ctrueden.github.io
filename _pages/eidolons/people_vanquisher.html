History

Ojo Lanre is an oni who, about a thousand years ago, rallied the Platypods, Echidnox, other oni and fey, and Gearnon tribe to take on the Tarrasque "infestation" of the temple. They were decimated, and his own people dubbed him "the Vanquisher" in mockery, since he "vanquished" his own people. The Platypods rejected that the temple should be protected at all anymore, and the Gearnon tribe adopted their fatalistic views held to this day. Eternally ashamed, the Vanquisher left the Wilds never to return.

He refuses to lay a hand against the nomads or any Fey creature, though it rarely comes up in the Wastes.
