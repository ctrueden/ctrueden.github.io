Races

We have adapted several races to the Pathfinder system, including:

  • Deva
  • Dragonborn
  • Warforged

 

The favored classes for various races are:

  • Deva: paladin and cleric
  • Dragonborn: fighter and paladin
  • Dwarf: cleric and swashbuckler
  • Genasi: element-dependent; ask if interested
