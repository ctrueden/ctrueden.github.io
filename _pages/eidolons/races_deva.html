Deva

Deva racial characteristics:

  • +2 Int, +2 Wis, -2 Con
  • Medium size
  • +2 racial bonus to Knowledge (history) and Knowledge (religion), and devas can make untrained skill checks with these two knowledges
  • Normal vision
  • Astral majesty: +1 to AC and saves versus divine sources
  • Astral resistance: resist 5 to damage from divine sources
  • Immortal origin: Your spirit is native to the astral sea, so you are considered an immortal creature for the purposes of effects that relate to creature origin
  • Memory of a thousand lifetimes: Use d8s instead of d6s for action points

When a deva is slain it is reincarnated at a temple or other holy place associated with the deity with whom it is tied.
