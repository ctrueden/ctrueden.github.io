Dragonborn

Dragonborn racial characteristics:

  • Average Height: 6' 2" - 6' 8"
  • Average Weight: 220 - 320 lbs
  • Ability Scores: +2 Str, -2 Int, +2 Cha
  • Size: Medium
  • Vision: Normal
  • Automatic Languages: Common, Draconic
  • Scary: +2 racial bonus to Intimidate
  • Dragon Blood: Dragonborn count as both humanoids and dragons for any effect related to race
  • Dragonborn Fury: +1 racial bonus to hit when current vitality is 5 or less
  • Draconic Heritage: +1 wound point
  • Dragon Breath: 3d10 + Con damage of associated element in a 30-foot cone, usable once per day (Reflex half DC 10 + half character level + Con)
