Dwarf/Eladrin

Halfbreed dwarf/eladrin have the following characteristics:

  • speed 30 (as elf)
  • +2 Dex, +2 Con, -2 Wis
  • Darkvision 60' (as dwarf)
  • Stonecunning (as dwarf)
  • Keen Senses: sight & sound (as elf)
  • Defensive Training (as dwarf)
  • Stability (as dwarf)
