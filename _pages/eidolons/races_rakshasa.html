Rakshasa

This version of the rakshasa has been adapted to be less powerful with a more playable level adjustment.

Rakshasa racial features as doppelganger except:

  • Replace "immunity to sleep and charm effects" with "SR 20 + character level, except that clerics and paladins count as double caster level for breaching it"
  • Add vulnerability to blessed crossbow bolt (always does wound damage rather than vitality)
  • Change base movement from 30 to 40
  • Add good Fort save bonus
  • Ability modifiers: +2 Str, +2 Dex, +4 Con, +2 Int, +2 Wis, +4 Cha
  • Add natural attacks: two claws and a bite
  • Add Abyssal as an automatic language
  • Level adjustment: +5 (total ECL 9)
