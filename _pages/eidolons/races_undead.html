Undead

Undead have a wound score as if their constitution were 10. All your hit dice are maximized d12s, meaning you have quite a bit more vitality, so you should be fairly hard to kill, in keeping with undead tradition.

No attack versus undead goes straight to wounds—all vitality must be depleted first. An undead who reaches zero wounds remaining is destroyed.
