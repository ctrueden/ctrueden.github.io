System: Pathfinder with modifications:

  • Merit system for additional character customization.
  • Modified magic system.
  • Wounds/vitality rather than hit points.
  • Minor actions and action points.
  • Starting level will be 7.

Setting: Even though we are using PF mechanics, the setting will incorporate many cosmological elements from 4E, such as 4E's reconceived planar structure. Regarding deities, we are using those from both the 3.5E and 4E Player's Handbooks (not the Pathfinder deities).

Favored classes: Whether you take a "favored class" has no mechanical effect.
