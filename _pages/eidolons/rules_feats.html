Feats

The following changes to Pathfinder feats are in effect:

 

Shatter Defenses

Your skill with your chosen weapon leaves opponents unable to defend themselves if you strike them when their defenses are already compromised.

Prerequisites: Weapon Focus, Dazzling Display, base attack bonus +6, proficiency with weapon.

Benefit: Any shaken, frightened, or panicked opponent hit by you this round is denied his Dexterity bonus to AC for your attacks until the end of your next turn. This includes any additional attacks you make this round.
