Skills

The following skill changes are in place from the Pathfinder system:

  1. Concentration is split from Spellcraft, and incorporates Autohypnosis.
    • Class skill for: bard, druid, geomancer, monk, paladin, ranger, runemage, swordsage
       
  2. Knowledge (martial), also known as Martial Lore from the Tome of Battle, is a knowledge of fighting styles, including their signature moves, strengths and weaknesses, cultural ties, and so on.
    • Class skill for: monk, rogue, swordsage, and any class with a full BAB progression
       
  3. Geomancy is a new skill for analyzing ley lines and nexuses. It has the following characteristics:
    • Induce prophecy while at a nexus
    • Assess the health of nexus or ley line energy
    • Determine distance and size of nearby nexuses by studying ley line flow
    • Identify geomancy powers as they are manifested, and while they are in effect
    • Class skill for: druid and geomancer
