Sahrit Arrives in Arx.  Having discover that Kas had "died" at the gates of town, he stays at a Delphian nobles' household that doubles as a bardic school while searching for Kas' sword.  Sahrit manages to get the matron of the household to ask him to join the noble youth of the house in their mission.  He is certain that the cargo being escorted is Kas' sword.  As a tribal army aproaches the cargo near the Arx outpost a giant quake transports Sahrit far away and replaces him with a withered looking woman on a firey steed.

After helping to escort a fleet of orcs through the teleporation portal, Rebin and Gwen leave Arx on a guard mission in order to avoid the pending investigation.

Za'har, Entry 1

Submitted by joel on Fri, 2009-09-18 16:38.

If what you seek is always in the last place you look, then you have chosen too soon to give up the search. -Grandmaster Egorin

Whenever I find myself frustrated by a task, I try to reflect on these words, gifted to me by my master. I must remember that truth is to be discovered in the entirety of one's journey, rather than merely its conclusion. Despite this, I have traveled many months, and feel no closer to any answers. Still, I have found a stalwart companion in Arwel, and a series of mysteries in young Haroun. If I posess the wisdom to remain attentive, I expect there will be much here for me to learn.

Arwel and I will be taking Haroun with us on our latest mercenary contract, the escort of a Delphian lineship. I admit a certain excitment in seeing one personally, though the vagueries of our contract concern me. Furthermore, Haroun recieved a powerful vision, of our protected cargo somehow finding a place in Arwel's posession, and of myself twisted, corrupted into the vile Rahshasa. I pray that so troubling a vision speaks merely of some metaphor, though the imagery leaves me on edge.

Within the settlement, as the lineship approached, the chaos of this world shook my resolve as a sizable quake struck, replacing one of the caravan guards with none other than General Isaline, though I fear even she may not be enough to fight the massed hordes that now approach us.
