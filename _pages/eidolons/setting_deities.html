Deities

* = depicted in a mural at the Temple of Sigil

% = appeared at the Temple of Sigil (though some claim these individuals were actually the Lords of Hell)

 

Various

Avandra - good - freedom, trade, adventure, travel, frontier, often worshipped in the free cities

Heironeous* - lawful good - valor, worshipped by great heroes and paladins of all cultures

Kord*% - neutral - storm god, lord of battle, worshipped by all cultures

Olidammara*% - chaotic neutral - trickster, thieves, worshipped by the kitsune

Pelor* - good - sun, summer, time, worshipped by "those who pursue good in its purest form"

 

Delphia

Boccob* - neutral - magic, knowledge, mainly worshipped by Delphians

Erathis% - lawful - great invention, founder of cities, author of laws, worshipped by Delphians

Garl Glittergold* - good - gnomes, joker, watchful protector, priceless gem, sparkling wit

Ioun* - neutral - knowledge, skill and prophecy, worshipped by Delphians

 

Durus

Bahamut% - lawful good - justice, protection, nobility, honor, worshipped in the Empire

Moradin*% - lawful good - artisans, miners, smiths, dwarves; imprisoned in a block of ice in Stygia; Rebin's father

Tiamat - evil - wealth, greed, envy, worshipped by chromatic dragons

Io* - neutral - supreme god of dragons, from whom Bahamut and Tiamat are aspects

 

The Fey

Corellon*% - neutral - spring, beauty, the arts, arcane magic, mostly worshipped by the Fey

Ehlonna% - good - goddess of woodlands, worshipped by those who dwell in the Feywood; daughter of Corellon and Sehanine?

Fharlanghn* - neutral - god of roads, travel, distance, horizons, worshipped by any who travel, particularly the nomads

Melora* - good - goddess of the wilderness, once worshipped by the Fey; believed deceased

Obad-Hai* - neutral - nature, wilderness and storms, worshipped by druids

Sehanine*% - neutral - moon, autumn, trickery, illusions, Lolth's sister, worshipped by trickster Fey and lycanthropes

Yondalla* - lawful good - halflings

 

Tribal Hordes

Bane* - evil - war and conquest, god of goblins, worshipped in the Hordes

Erythnul*% - chaotic evil - slaughter, panic, ogres

Gruumsh*% - chaotic evil - destruction, barbarian hordes, orcs

Hextor* - lawful evil - tyranny, worshipped by pit fighters and gladiators (especially in Ahote)

 

The Abyss

Demogorgon - chaotic evil - self-styled king of the Abyss

Graz'zt - chaotic evil - one of the princes of the Abyss; believed deceased, slain by the Lady of Pain


Underdark and the Hells

Asmodeus% - lawful evil - tyranny and domination, Lord of the Nine Hells

Baalzebul - lawful evil - lord of Maladomini, the seventh Hell; was once the first Emperor of Durus

Levistus - lawful evil - lord of Stygia, the fifth Hell; Moradin's eidolon

Lolth* - chaotic evil - shadow, lies, spiders, scheming, treachery

 

Death and vengeance

Kiaransalee - unknown alignment - the true name of the Raven Queen?

Nerull% - evil - death and withering

Raven Queen - neutral - death, spinner of fate, patron of winter, worshipped by those with a particular interest in death; referred to by Pelor as the Lady of Pain

St. Cuthbert - lawful - justice and retribution, worshipped by the wronged

Tharizdun - unknown alignment - the Chained God; fate, destiny, worshipped by humans

Wee Jas* - lawful - death, magic and solitude, worshipped by those who hunt undead

Vance Doubain – neutral evil - Vecna's eidolon, a necromancer lich; Sahrit's father

Vecna* - evil - necromancy, secrets, forbidden knowledge, worshipped by necromancers
