Places

Name sources: Empire and Delphia, Fey

 

Major geographic features

The Great Wastes – vast desert in the center of the known world; home to the tribal hordes

The Abundant Steppes – an expanse of grassland surrounding the Wastes, with rivers and lakes aplenty

The Feywood – further out from the Wastes than the Steppes lies the Feywood, a wood that grows more magical and more dangerous the further in one travels

The Frozen Wood – a section of the Feywood far to the east, where temperatures are much colder than elsewhere; the trees there are supernaturally resistant to heat

The Wilds – deep within the Feywood, it is said that there lies a jungle, as mysterious as it is deadly; few if any of those who set foot in the Wilds ever return, and no one has dared to establish a permanent settlement there

The Jotunfjell – a large mountain range mostly encompassed by the Empire

 

Rivers and lakes

Naerdiel (nyre-dee-ell, "lady of sorrows") – A great lake in the west Steppes. In Fey legend, a woman died of sorrow and cried a lake of tears

Mordollwen (More-dole-wehn, "Dark as night") – A great swamp along the north shore of Lake Naerdiel. Some say it is cursed. It is rumored that a black dragon, or worse, dwells there

Ethiriel (Eh-theer-ee-ell) – Great river flowing northwest from Lake Naerdiel

Rossarinya (Rohs-sar-inn-yah, "Morning Dew") – Another great river flowing west-southwest from Lake Naerdiel

Earenniel (Ay-are-ehn-nee-ell, "Daughter of the Ocean") – A smaller lake in the Feywood, northwest of Lake Naerdiel

Celunibeniel (Kehl-oo-nih-behn-ee-ell, "small stream") – A smaller river

Tethys – A massive east-flowing river that runs through the heart of the Durus Empire

Yára (Yahr-ah, "Ancient") – The sole river, south-bound, flowing from the Wilds to the north

 

The Durus Empire (martial ideologues)

Elyria – major SW territory (and capital city thereof) of the Empire
Manavendra – Elyrian city in the wastes; home to Za'hran
Hanfield – small town in Elyrian territory, Isaline's birthplace
Celapelai – one of the Marquis of Elyria's oldest estates, where Isaline was raised
Blackdale – a city subservient to Elyria for the past century; ruled by Soren van Wagram, the Duke of Blackdale
Eloyes (formerly the free city of Llanelli) – captured by the Empire against its will one month past, when an ogre-led orc tribe unexpectedly captured a nearby fortress and threatened the city; Isaline was instrumental in the Empire's success; Biger originally hailed from an alternate Llanelli that had chosen to join with Delphia some years past

Midgard – major NW territory of the Empire
Asgard – the capital city of Midgard
Draupnir – second city (after Asgard) to be built along the edges of the Wastes, to keep out invaders
Arkinheim – "the land of the eternal king" – first outpost built to mount an offensive against the tribes
Heimdall – Midgard city farthest into the Wastes, built to prevent any large incursion from Ahote from threatening Asgard

Niflheim – major NE territory (and capital city thereof) of the Empire; colder than other regions, due to its proximity to the Frozen Wood
Arve – city in northern Niflheim; built just inside the Frozen Wood; one of the only imperial cities built primarily out of wood
Ystradfelte – city in the southern end of Niflheim, nestled between two rivers

Valhalla – major S/SE territory (and capital city thereof) of the Empire
Trimsaran – city in Valhalla, in the mountains
Jorvikston – fortress city along the southern edge of Valhalla

Olympia – central territory of the Empire
Alexandria – capital city of the Empire, in Olympia

 

Free cities

Ahote – a sprawling Horde city in the Wastes, home to many tribes, who manage to get along occasionally

Etheriel's Fork – at the Ethiriel river delta

Freeport – on the southern bank of Lake Naerdiel, home to gnollish rangers

Peril's Edge – near the northern Wilds

Tat'enda – home to Arwel, near the Wastes, sandwiched between the Empire, the tribal hordes, and Arx, a Delphian city; now Ordered

Dorsidhion ("land-peace") – on the Rossarinya river, near the Feywood

Azure Sands – at the border of the Great Wastes

 

Delphia (the technoprophets)

Sollicitus – first city of Delphia, and the capital city, deepest in the forest (but still not to the jungle region); largest production center for warforged; where Ed was made

Intempestivus – second city of Delphia, also fairly deep in the forest, home to largest Delphian academy (more eladrin than gnome)

Oraculum – third city of Delphia; largest city of the six; in the grasslands; encompasses two nexuses; home to the most powerful geomancers

Terminus – fourth city of Delphia; north of the wastes; fairly deep in the forest; old town is known as Anaya; particularly known for its innovation and juxtaposition of magic and technology

Arx – fifth city of Delphia; heavily fortified city deep in the wastes; joined with Delphia ~100 years ago; home to Rupert and Priscilla; travel to Arx is currently restricted to technoprophet allies only.

Factum – sixth city of Delphia, also in the wastes; most recently constructed and ambitious city to date; oldtown is known as Ashbury; Simone's eladrin father originated here.

 

The Fey (mystic nomads)

Baenallia – "Harvesters" – Nomadic tribe touring many western areas, including the Wilds, the Feywood, the Steppes, the Wastes, Lake Naerdiel and the cursed swamp Mordollwen; known for their harvesting skills

Gearnon – "Pacifists" – Nomadic tribe traveling mostly the northwestern Feywood; strictest proponents of fatalism and advocates of the flow

T'Sen – "Warriors" – Nomadic tribe traveling the northwestern Wastes and northern Steppes, known for its warrior culture; the southern tip of their migration path seems them visiting [X], one of the four major cities still independent; Simone's elven mother originated with them

Idhrenion – "Watchers" – Nomadic tribe traveling from Solicitus north across the [X] and [X] rivers to the desert city of [X], one of the four that still remains independent; known for their vigilance against threats to the nomad lifestyle

Haerion – "Recluses" – Nomadic tribe exclusively traveling the southwestern Feywood. One end of their migration sees them making a brief stop at Intempestivus to visit with their fey cousins, but they avoid contact with other cultures otherwise.

Fochlucan – A Fey settlement in the wastes

 

The Underdark

Tane'klenya – Largest drow settlement beneath the Empire

Bael'turath – Largest tiefling settlement beneath the Empire
