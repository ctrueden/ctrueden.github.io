Pax Artificium - Gaia's Order

  • Main
  • Characters
  • Story

This organization, led by a pixie named Everlily Nyx, represents the interests of the living earth, whom they call "Gaia." Although they seem to be genuinely concerned about the future of the planet, their hatred of humans coupled with their "ends justify the means" attitude thus far have portrayed them in a poor light.

At least one member, a siren named Marissa, has implied that Nyx is the one driving the group to such drastic action, and that many among them disagree with her methods.
