Banks is of average height, with brown hair in a crew cut. Like his partner Summers, he rarely removes his hi-tech sunglasses. He is a weapons expert, and former first officer of the CIA cell led by James Rex. He studied psionics under Rex, blending the techniques into his own unique style of martial arts.

He and Banks were last seen on the small island in the Bermuda Triangle. After Noj and Sitruc appeared to stop Rex's plan to destroy the obelisk there, and Rex was transformed into a wraith, they disappeared...
