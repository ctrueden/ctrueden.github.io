Bubbles is badass and tough-talking (and certainly not cute or cuddly). He is the leader of the Fantastic Five. Rumor has it that he once had ties to the mob, but that he has moved on. He drives a limo, occasionally acting as a chauffer for high-ranking Magitech personnel.
