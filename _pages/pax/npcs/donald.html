Donald was a normal guy until he went through a mid-life crisis when his wife left him. Seeking guidance, he went walking through the park one day, and heard Sara speaking about Janell. Though he was at first amused, something about the teaching fascinated him, and ultimately he joined the Way of Janell as a devout believer in its principles.

Along with Brother Philip, he insists he received orders from Janell herself to track down and kill Daniel Miller, because his existence supposedly threatened all humankind. They were unsuccessful in this attempt, and are currently in jail serving their sentence.
