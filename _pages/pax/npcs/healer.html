The man once known as Fox Arthur, crushed by the loss of his wife, left Proto-Magitech behind, deciding to travel the world for a time. He ended up wandering Asia, where he encountered many people who were poor and sick. He decided to use his mystical talents to heal their wounds, and before long, a sort of cult sprang up around his miraculous abilities. They called him the Great Healer.

But magic always has its price, and after years of germination, the diseases he had healed in others gained an intelligence of their own, taking over his mind and body, replacing his compassion with twisted evil.

One goal remained: to destroy the man who was once his son. Fueled by pure hatred, he wielded an evil magic pure enough to defeat Cid's tranquility. He recruited many to his new cause, infecting dead criminals with his disease, allowing it to gain strength by feasting upon their tainted souls.

Although the Wednesday group destroyed the Great Healer's body once, he somehow regenerated and returned once more—this time, accompanied by a host of robed individuals apparently from the Way of Janell (though they were thought to have been destroyed when Pax Artificium was nuked).

Posing as of one of the Janellers, the Great Healer confronted Cid and stabbed him. Though Jack and William Moss subsequently destroyed him using the antigen James Rex created, Cid did not survive the experience either.
