Kodiak was in the military for many years. After that, he became a hostage negotiator for a time, a job at which he excelled. They say he tired of the mediocre pay, however, and decided to put his negotation skills to good use robbing banks and other high-cash establishments. Now he is wanted in several states but has still never been convicted.
