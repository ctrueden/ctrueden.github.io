Lefty is an obnoxious but fun-loving brat. She has dyed her hair pink, and is always chewing gum. She jets around town on her high-tech skates. Although Lefty is a member of the Fantastic Five, she disappeared from Pax Artificium four months ago, leaving only a note saying that she had to take care of some things, and would be back later. Lastly, it appears that Anna may be her mother.
