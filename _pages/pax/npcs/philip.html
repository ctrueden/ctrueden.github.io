Philip was dissatisfied with his life, thinking he was on a dead end course. He turned to the Way of Janell to get a fresh start at life.

Along with Brother Donald, he insists he received orders from Janell herself to track down and kill Daniel Miller, because his existence supposedly threatened all humankind. They were unsuccessful in this attempt, and are currently in jail serving their sentence.
