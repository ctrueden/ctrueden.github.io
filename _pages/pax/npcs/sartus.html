Sartus was once somewhat of a scholar among the elves, taking it upon himself to chronicle the events surrounding the construction of the ancient obelisk. Now, like most of his people, his body is long dead, and his spirit has been fused with the body of a deceased human, yielding a wraith.

Though Sartus and Benek (friends during life) served their king, Janus Ilaris, faithfully, they grew increasingly concerned when it became clear that Janus's sole purpose for their people was to exact revenge upon the human descendants of the ones responsible for their people's demise.

When Janus himself became bound in wraith form and was no longer his own master, Sartus and Benek were uncertain what to do next. But once they learned that their people had been miraculously born anew within the barren womb of Sara Cassenti, they realized a better, more harmonious route lay ahead. Even if their spirits are twisted beyond redemption, Sartus and Benek have vowed to do whatever is necessary to protect Sara's twins, to ensure the rebirth and survival of their once-proud race.
