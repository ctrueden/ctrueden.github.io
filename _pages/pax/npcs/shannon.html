Shannon is an intelligent young Irish woman who works for Magitech as a technician. Though fairly shy while at work, she does her share of partying during her off hours. She has had a crush on her boss Liam Connor for some time, but didn't work up the courage to say anything until recently. Once Shannon sets her mind to something, though, she is tenacious.

Since she convinced Liam to go out with her on New Years Eve, Shannon got caught up in the latest set of misadventures. After being abducted by a government S.W.A.T. team and locked into a van with the Fabulous Four, she disappeared, and her current whereabouts are unknown...
