Summers is shorter than average, with dirty blonde hair tied back with unruly bangs. Like his partner Banks, he rarely removes his hi-tech sunglasses. He is a computer expert, and former second officer of the CIA cell led by James Rex. He studied psionics under Rex, using them to blast his enemies from a distance.

He and Summers were last seen on the small island in the Bermuda Triangle. After Noj and Sitruc appeared to stop Rex's plan to destroy the obelisk there, and Rex was transformed into a wraith, they disappeared...
