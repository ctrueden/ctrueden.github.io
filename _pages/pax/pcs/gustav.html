Gustav Weiner was chief research scientist in StratCo. He was given free reign over his department, because his miraculous abilities provided substantial income for the company. After the coup, Gustav aligned himself with Rixmann's group. Obeying his orders with alacrity, Gustav met his untimely end in an attempt to kill the group of misfits he'd been gadding about with.
