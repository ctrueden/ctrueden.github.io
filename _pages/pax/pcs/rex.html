Rex Howell is very well-known in the circle of antique collectors, for his unusual possession of some value. He is known under the codename "Carnivore" in less savory elements of society, as a hitman of substantial merit. He always accomplishes his goals, slaughtering men with ease and skill. Carnivore has recently been hired by a man named "Butler," and has found himself reentangled with the Order of Gaia, a society he left in disfavor.
