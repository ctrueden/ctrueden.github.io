PowerGame 2K1 - DM Rulings and Questions

Q: What happens if you Shapechange into creature with the Swallow Whole ability, swallow something, then Shapechange into something smaller?
A: (Curt) You can only Shapechange if it makes sense. If the swallowed creature or object wouldn't fit in the new target form's stomach (or area in the case of an ooze), the Shapechange fails.

Q: Do Ethereal Jaunt or other plane shifting spells (besides Plane Shift) help you escape from a Maze?
A: (Curt) Nope.

Q: Can you Dimension Door from inside a Prismatic Wall to outside one?
A: (Curt) Nope.

Q: Is reading a scroll prevented by being inside the area of an Insect Plague?
A: (Jason) Nope.
